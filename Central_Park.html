Central Park

Welcome to the Big Apple's Central Park!

Central Park is an urban park in Manhattan. Central Park is the most visited urban park in the United States. Central Park offers hundreds of special attractions for solo explorers and families across its 843 acres: green meadows, sprawling waters, gardens, unique bridges, performance centers, educational facilities and classical architecture.

Park Type: State Park
State: New York
