Timucuan National Preserve

Welcome to the Sunshine State's Timucuan National Preserve!

Timucuan is located in northeastern Florida along the Atlantic coast. We are a Ecological and Historic National Preserve featuring 46,000 acres of wetlands, waterways, and other natural habitats. Additionally Fort Caroline National Memorial and the Kingsley Plantation are located in the park.

Park Type: National Preserve
State: Florida
