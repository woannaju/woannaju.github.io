Wild and Scenic River

A Wild and Scenic River is a river in the United States that is preserved for possessing outstandingly remarkable scenic, recreational, geological, fish and wildlife, historic, cultural, or other similar values. These rivers, or sections of rivers, are preserved in their free-flowing condition and are not dammed or otherwise impeded.

There are 159 Wild and Scenic Rivers in the US with the most popular being the Rio Grande River in Texas.
National Monument Name Location Description
Rio Grande River Texas The Rio Grande River is one of the principal rivers in the southwest United States and northern Mexico and serves as a natural border between the US and Mexico.
