Anza-Borrego Desert State Park

Welcome to the Golden State's Anza-Borrego Desert State Park!

Anza-Borrego is located in southern California and is a part of the Colorado Desert.We are a state park featuring 110 miles of hiking trails, native palm oases, desert bighorn sheep, and slot canyons all within our 600,000 acres.
